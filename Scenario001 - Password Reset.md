# Scenario 001 - Password Reset

## Customer

Hi, I forgot my password and I can't access my account.

---

## Agent

Hello!

I'd be happy to help you regain access to your account.

Please click **Forgot Password** on the login page and enter your registered email address.

You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes.

---

## Customer

I haven't received the email.

---

## Agent

Please check your Spam or Junk folder.

Also confirm that you're using the same email address you used during registration.

If you still don't receive it after 10 minutes, let us know and we'll investigate further.

---

## Resolution

Password successfully reset.
